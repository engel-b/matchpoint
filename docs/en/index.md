# Why MatchPoint?

MatchPoint brings modern table tennis scoreboard to every device – from smartphones to tablets. As a Progressive Web App, it is free, works completely offline and can be installed in just a few seconds.

The application runs entirely on your device, requires no cloud services and supports freely configurable input devices. This makes it ideal for casual games, training sessions, club nights and small tournaments.

The focus is on fast and intuitive operation, a large and easy-to-read display, and support for configurable input devices – so you can keep your attention where it belongs: on the table.
