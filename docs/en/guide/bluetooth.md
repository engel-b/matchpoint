# Bluetooth Buttons

MatchPoint supports Bluetooth HID buttons.

Open the app settings and use **Remote buttons** to learn custom keys.

Typical supported keys are:

- `PageUp`
- `PageDown`
- `AudioVolumeUp`
- `AudioVolumeDown`

Volume keys may also change the device volume depending on the operating system. If possible, use a page-turning or keyboard mode on your remote.
